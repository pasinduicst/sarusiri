About Saru Siri

Your neighborhood grocery store since 2010

At Saru Siri, we believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, high-quality groceries at fair prices. Founded by local entrepreneur Saman Perera, our store has been serving the community for over a decade with a commitment to freshness, quality, and friendly service.

We source our produce daily from local farmers and suppliers, ensuring you get the freshest fruits, vegetables, and essentials. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you find exactly what you need.
